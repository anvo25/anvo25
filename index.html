An Vo

MS student @ Korea Advanced Institute of Science & Technology (KAIST)

I am a second-year MS student at KAIST, working with Professor Daeyoung Kim and Professor Anh Totti Nguyen. My MS program is fully funded by Hyundai CMK Global Scholarship.

Prior to join KAIST, I obtained my BS degree as the valedictorian at University of Information Technology (UIT), Vietnam National University - Ho Chi Minh City (VNU-HCM) in 2023, where I worked with Dr. Ngoc Hoang Luong.

My research interests include LLMs/VLMs, AutoML, Evolutionary Computation, and Optimization.
